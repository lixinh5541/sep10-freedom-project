Animation

Context

What is animation?

  • Animation can be a form of entertainment, advertisement, and even education
  • Animation is a technique used to make images feel like they are moving.
  • Animators can use a device or paper to draw
  • Animation is extremely popular all animes and many movies are made using animation like: Shrek, Inside out, and Toy story

Why is animation important?

  • Some business can use animation for advertisement because it can attract customers
  • Animators can gain money from people watching their animation
  • Some animators use animation to educate students, by giving them a visual demonstration
