Animation

Context

What is animation?

  • Animation can be a form of entertainment, advertisement, and even education
  • Animation is a technique used to make images feel like they are moving.
  • Animators can use a device or paper to draw
  • Animation is extremely popular all animes and many movies are made using animation like: Shrek, Inside out, and Toy story

Why is animation important?

  • Some business can use animation for advertisement because it can attract customers
  • Animators can gain money from people watching their animation
  • Some animators use animation to educate students, by giving them a visual demonstration

Animation Hardware

Animation light box

A device used to light up a transparent surface, allowing animators to see through multiple layers of paper when drawing or tracing frames.

Camcorder

A video camera but are explicitly used to record videos.

Scanner

A device that capture images from a paper you put in and uploads them to your computer for editing.

Stylus pen

A stylus pen is a pen-shaped device used to interact with other devices with touchscreens, especially on devices like tablets and smartphones

Graphic tablet

A device that allows people to draw using a stylus or digital pen on its surface.

3D printer

A machine that creates a physical object from a 3D model, and is used in 3D animation to create a model of what a animator wants.

Futuristic ideas

3D animations headset

  • Viewers could watch animation in 3d with a headset
  • Make them feel like they are actually there watching the animation
  • Some animations can implement decisions that the viewer can do, that change the ending and what happens in the animation
  • The headset can have headphones that play the background music and sound effects

Copy pen

  • This pen will help animators animate things faster by making them able to copy their work from previous frames
  • For example, an animator can copy a soccer ball and move it slightly with each frame so they don't have to keep drawing the soccer ball.
  • Animators will select the thing they want to copy by drawing a frame around the object
  • The pen can have different modes that you can switch between using buttons on the pen, and these modes can make the object change properties like size, and rotating the object

Animator glasses

  • A big problem with animation is that it's stressful, and takes a mental and physical toll on the animator, this glasses will fix these problems
  • Animation projects take a long time usually on a computer which can strain the eyes, these glasses can have a special feature which keeps the brightness of the screen, but reduces the strain on the eyes
  • Since animation projects take a long time, these glasses can have a timer to show how long a animator has worked for, to ensure that the animator is taking breaks in between work
  • These glasses can have a feedback mechanism where fans and supporters of a animator can give feedback or words of encouragement and the message would be sent to the glasses for the animator to look at
